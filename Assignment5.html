Raspberries

HEALTHY FRUIT

Nowadays you can buy raspberries all year round. They are at their best in the summer, of course. Delicious with yogurt at breakfast, but also straight out of the hand as a snack.

Raspberries contain unique antioxidants called eliagitannins Eliagitannies can be found in small amounts in strawberries and in some medicinal herbs. They prevent cells from being damaged. but there are even more good things in raspberries.

  • Vitamin C and B
  • folic acid
  • Copper and iron

Vitamin C

The tasty pink-red fruits are full of vitamins C. You need that for good resistance and sufficient energy in addition, it ensures a well-functioning nervous system and healthy bones, teeth, skin, hair and blood vessels.

folic acid

In addition, raspberries are a source of folic acid. This is also called vitamin B11 and is indispensable for the body. it helps in the production of red blood cells in the bone marrow.
